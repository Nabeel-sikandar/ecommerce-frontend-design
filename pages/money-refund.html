Money Refund Policy

We stand behind everything we sell. 30-day hassle-free returns guaranteed.

Home Help Center Money Refund

30-Day Return Window

Return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund — no questions asked.

  • Item must be unused and in original packaging
  • Tags and labels must be intact
  • Original receipt or order number required

Buyer Protection

You're covered if the item doesn't arrive, arrives damaged, or doesn't match the description.

  • Full refund for damaged items
  • Full refund if item not received
  • Partial refund for minor discrepancies

Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons.

  • Perishable goods
  • Digital downloads
  • Customized/personalized items
  • Opened hygiene products

How to Request a Refund

1

Log Into Your Account

Go to your profile and navigate to "My Orders" section.

2

Select the Order

Find the order you want to return and click "Request Refund".

3

Choose Reason

Select the reason for your return from the available options.

4

Ship the Item

Pack the item and ship it using the prepaid label we email you.

5

Get Your Refund

Refund is processed within 3–5 business days of receiving the item.

Refund Timeline

Day 1

Refund Request Submitted

You submit your refund request via the website or by emailing us.

Day 1–2

Request Reviewed

Our team reviews your request and sends you a prepaid return shipping label.

Day 3–7

Item Received at Warehouse

We receive and inspect the returned item at our fulfillment center.

Day 8–10

Refund Processed

Refund is approved and processed to your original payment method.

Day 10–14

Money in Your Account

Funds appear in your bank or card account (timing depends on your bank).
